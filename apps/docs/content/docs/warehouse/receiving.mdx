The receiving workflow processes incoming deliveries against purchase orders.

## Workflow

1. **Search** for the purchase order by number
2. For each PO item, record:
   - **Received quantity** — Actual amount delivered
   - **Quality status** — Approved, Needs Inspection, or Rejected
   - **Discrepancy type** — Short shipment, overage, damage, wrong item
3. **Complete receiving** — Finalizes the PO and updates inventory

## Quality Inspection

Three quality statuses:
- **Approved** — Item passes inspection, added to available stock
- **Needs Inspection** — Item is quarantined pending review
- **Rejected** — Item is refused, not added to stock

## Receiving Reports

Analytics on receiving performance by supplier (response times, quality rates, discrepancy frequency).
