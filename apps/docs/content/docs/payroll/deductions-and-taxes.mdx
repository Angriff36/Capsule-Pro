The payroll engine (`@repo/payroll-engine`) handles all deduction and tax calculations with high precision.

## Tax Calculations

### Federal Taxes
- 2024 tax brackets for single and married filing statuses
- Progressive bracket calculation

### State Taxes
Supported states:
- California (CA)
- New York (NY)
- Texas (TX) — no state income tax
- Florida (FL) — no state income tax
- Washington (WA) — no state income tax
- Pennsylvania (PA)
- Illinois (IL)
- Ohio (OH)

### Payroll Taxes
- **Social Security** — 6.2% with $168,600 annual cap
- **Medicare** — 1.45% base + 0.9% additional Medicare tax on high earners

## Deductions

11 deduction types with pre-tax or post-tax classification:

| Type | Tax Treatment |
|------|--------------|
| Health Insurance | Pre-tax |
| Dental Insurance | Pre-tax |
| Vision Insurance | Pre-tax |
| 401(k) | Pre-tax |
| HSA | Pre-tax |
| FSA | Pre-tax |
| Roth 401(k) | Post-tax |
| Life Insurance | Post-tax |
| Garnishment | Post-tax |
| Union Dues | Post-tax |
| Other | Configurable |

## Tip Pool Allocation

Tips can be pooled and distributed by:
- **Hours worked** — Proportional to hours
- **Headcount** — Equal split
- **Fixed shares** — Custom allocation percentages

## Precision

All calculations use `Decimal.js` with 20-digit precision and ROUND_HALF_UP rounding to prevent floating-point errors.
