Purchase orders track procurement from suppliers through receiving.

## PO Lifecycle

Managed through Manifest IR commands:

1. `create` — Draft a new PO
2. `submit` — Submit for approval
3. `approve` / `reject` — Approval decision
4. `mark-ordered` — PO has been sent to supplier
5. `mark-received` — Goods have arrived
6. `cancel` — Cancel the PO

## PO Items

Each PO contains line items:
- `create` / `update` / `remove` — Item management
- Each item tracks ordered quantity, received quantity, and unit price

## Receiving Workflow

When goods arrive:

1. Look up the PO
2. For each item, record:
   - **Received quantity** — How much actually arrived
   - **Quality status** — Approved, Needs Inspection, or Rejected
   - **Discrepancy type** — If quantities don't match
3. Complete receiving — creates inventory transactions and updates stock levels

## Suppliers

Suppliers are managed through Manifest commands:
- `create` / `update` / `deactivate`
