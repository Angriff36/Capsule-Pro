---
title: Leads
description: Lead capture, qualification, and conversion to clients.
---

Leads represent potential clients who haven't yet been converted. The lead lifecycle tracks them from initial capture through qualification to conversion.

## Manifest Commands

- `create` — Capture a new lead
- `update` — Update lead information
- `disqualify` — Mark a lead as not viable
- `archive` — Archive an inactive lead
- `convert-to-client` — Convert a qualified lead into a full client record

## Conversion

When a lead is converted to a client, the system creates a new client record with the lead's information pre-populated. The lead record is archived and linked to the new client for history.
